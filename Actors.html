NTR

Junior NTR, whose full name is Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao Jr., is a celebrated Indian actor, playback singer, and television presenter known for his work in the Telugu film industry. Born on May 20, 1983, in Hyderabad, he is the grandson of the legendary actor, filmmaker, and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, Nandamuri Taraka Rama Rao (NTR). He began his acting career as a child artist in Bala Ramayanam (1996) and made his debut as a lead actor with Ninnu Choodalani (2001). Junior NTR gained stardom with hits like Student No. 1 (2001), Aadi (2002), and Simhadri (2003), and later delivered notable performances in films such as Yamadonga (2007), Temper (2015), Janatha Garage (2016), and Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava (2018). His portrayal of Komaram Bheem in the globally acclaimed film RRR (2022) brought him international recognition, especially with the film's song Naatu Naatu winning an Academy Award for Best Original Song. Known for his charismatic screen presence, exceptional dancing skills, and versatility, Junior NTR has won numerous accolades, including Filmfare Awards South, Nandi Awards, and SIIMA Awards. Outside of films, he hosted the Telugu version of Bigg Boss in 2017, earning praise for his engaging demeanor. Married to Lakshmi Pranathi and a father of two, Junior NTR continues to be a dominant force in Indian cinema and a beloved figure among fans.

Allu Arjun

Allu Arjun is a popular Indian actor known for his dynamic performances in the Telugu film industry. Born on April 8, 1983, in Chennai, he is the son of producer Allu Aravind and a member of the influential Allu family in Tollywood. Allu Arjun made his debut as a lead actor with Gangotri (2003) and gained fame with hits like Arya (2004), Bunny (2005), Desamuduru (2007), and Race Gurram (2014). Renowned for his extraordinary dancing skills and stylish on-screen presence, he is often referred to as the "Stylish Star." His career reached new heights with the blockbuster Pushpa: The Rise (2021), earning him pan-Indian acclaim. Allu Arjun has received several awards, including Filmfare Awards South and Nandi Awards, and remains one of the most beloved actors in Indian cinema.

Mahesh Babu

Mahesh Babu is a leading Indian actor and producer known for his work in the Telugu film industry. Born on August 9, 1975, in Chennai, he is the son of veteran actor Krishna and began his acting career as a child artist in films like Needa (1979). Mahesh made his debut as a lead actor with Raja Kumarudu (1999) and gained widespread recognition with hits like Murari (2001), Okkadu (2003), Pokiri (2006), and Srimanthudu (2015). Known for his charming screen presence and versatility, he is often referred to as the "Prince of Tollywood." Mahesh has received numerous accolades, including Filmfare Awards South and Nandi Awards, and is also celebrated for his philanthropic work. He is married to former actress Namrata Shirodkar and continues to be one of the most influential figures in Indian cinema.
